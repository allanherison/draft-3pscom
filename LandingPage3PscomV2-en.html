3PSCOM - Polarization, Populism and Post-Truth

Who We Are

We are an international network of researchers from more than 20 countries that study social and political debates in social media with big data techniques and social network analysis.

Our Aim

We analyze three structural phenomena in democratic societies: Polarization, Populism and Post-truth (the three P's). Specifically, we focus on digital public debate processes during electoral campaigns and social discussions.

  • Define digital polarization, online populism and post-truth in theoretical terms.
  • Develop Social Network Analysis tools to measure the three phenomena.
  • Analyze these processes in case studies across America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania.
